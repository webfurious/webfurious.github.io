Installation Instructions

1

Download the APK

Tap the download button and save the APK file to your device. It'll appear in your Downloads folder.

2

Allow Unknown Sources

Since this isn't from the Play Store yet, Android will ask for permission. Go to:

Settings → Apps → Special app access → Install unknown apps → select your browser → Allow
3

Install the APK

Open your Downloads folder, tap the StudioFlow APK file, and follow the installation prompts. It takes about 10 seconds.

4

Create Your Account

Open StudioFlow and create a free account. Your data is stored securely in Firebase. Once your Pro subscription is activated you'll have full access.

5

Send Me Feedback

Use the app for at least a week and note anything broken, confusing, or missing. Every bug report helps make StudioFlow better.

Security Note

Android may warn that this app is from an unknown source. This is normal for apps not distributed through the Play Store. The APK is built and signed by me. Feel free to scan it with any antivirus app for peace of mind.
